Your All-In-One Business Solution

What is Odoo/OpenERP?

Odoo, originally known as Tiny ERP and later OpenERP, is a comprehensive suite of open-source business applications. It integrates a wide range of functions—from CRM and sales to accounting and manufacturing—into a single, unified platform designed to streamline operations and boost efficiency.

Compare Odoo Editions

Why Odoo ERP?

Odoo provides an unmatched combination of flexibility, power, and affordability. It's an all-in-one solution that eliminates the need for multiple disjointed applications, offering a seamless and integrated experience that grows with your business.

Comprehensive & Integrated

With thousands of apps, Odoo covers all business needs in one stop. Information flows seamlessly between modules, eliminating redundant data entry.

Affordable & Scalable

Start with what you need and add apps as you grow. The free Community edition is ideal for startups, while Enterprise scales to any size at a competitive price.

Highly Customizable

Odoo's open-source model allows deep customization. The Odoo Studio app even makes personalization possible without writing any code.

Odoo All Versions Features Comparison

From Tiny ERP to a global business management powerhouse, Odoo's journey is one of continuous innovation.

2005

Tiny ERP is Born

Fabien Pinckaers develops the first version, laying the foundation for an open-source ERP.

2008

Rebranded as OpenERP

The name changes to reflect its expanded capabilities and growing community.

2014

The Birth of Odoo (v8)

Rebranded to Odoo, expanding beyond ERP with a Website Builder, eCommerce, and POS.

2015

Odoo Enterprise Launched (v9)

Introduction of a dual-license model: a free Community edition and a paid Enterprise edition with advanced features.

2022-2025

Modernization & AI

v16: Focused on performance boosts.
v17: Complete UI overhaul for better user experience.
v18: Expanded automation and mobile-first tools.
v19 (Upcoming): Deep AI integration, marketing revamp, and ESG tracking.

      Industrial Security Compliance

      Odoo is built with security at its core and can be configured to meet the requirements of various industrial security frameworks, ensuring your data remains protected.

      PCI DSS Compliance

      For businesses handling credit card payments, Odoo integrates with PCI DSS compliant payment gateways. This ensures that sensitive cardholder data is processed securely, reducing the risk of data breaches and helping you meet industry standards.

      HIPAA Compliance

      For healthcare organizations, Odoo's robust access control, audit logging, and data encryption features provide the foundation to build a HIPAA-compliant environment. With proper configuration and hosting, Odoo helps protect sensitive patient health information (PHI).
