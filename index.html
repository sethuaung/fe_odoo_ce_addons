      The Evolution of Odoo

      From Tiny ERP to a global business management powerhouse.

      2005

      Tiny ERP is Born

      Fabien Pinckaers starts to develop his first software product, Tiny ERP.

      2008

      Rebranded as OpenERP

      The software is renamed to OpenERP, reflecting its open-source nature and expanded capabilities.

      2014

      The Birth of Odoo

      With the release of version 8, OpenERP is rebranded to Odoo. This marks a shift beyond traditional ERP with the addition of a Website Builder, eCommerce, and Point of Sale apps.

      2015

      Odoo Enterprise is Launched

      Odoo 9 introduces the split between a free Community edition and a paid Enterprise edition, offering more advanced features and support.

      Present

      Continuous Innovation

      Odoo continues to release a new version annually, constantly improving performance, usability, and adding new applications to its ever-growing ecosystem.
